Hi, my name is Thiago! I was born and raised in Toledo a small town in the state of Paraná located in south Brazil. Since child I was amazed by computers and programming languages, my first website framed HTML with lots of animated gifs developed using Microsoft FrontPage 2000 around my 10 years old.
